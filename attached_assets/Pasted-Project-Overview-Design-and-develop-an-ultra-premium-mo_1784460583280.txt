Project Overview

Design and develop an ultra-premium, modern, luxury beauty salon website for Rupaliz Beauty World.

The website should deliver the feeling of a high-end salon and beauty studio through elegant layouts, cinematic animations, smooth scrolling, refined typography, and immersive visuals.

The experience should feel comparable to award-winning luxury beauty and wellness websites, while maintaining a unique identity.

Overall Feel

The website should evoke elegance, confidence, and relaxation. Motion should be subtle and polished, with cinematic transitions, smooth scrolling, premium typography, spacious layouts, and high-quality visuals. Every interaction should reinforce the perception of a modern luxury beauty destination, encouraging visitors to book services or enroll in the academy.
